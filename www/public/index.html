Basset Hounds

Welcome to my website dedicated to basset hounds! Originally bred as hunting dogs, they are known for their short legs and long ears.

Characteristics

  • Loud howls
  • Modern bassets hunt for cheeseburgers, chicken, steak, and bacon.
